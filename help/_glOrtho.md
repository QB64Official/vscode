## _glOrtho
---

### The _glOrtho statement multiplies the current matrix by an orthographic matrix.

#### SYNTAX

`_glOrtho GLdouble left , GLdouble right , GLdouble bottom , GLdouble top , GLdouble zNear , GLdouble zFar`
