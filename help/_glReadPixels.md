## _glReadPixels
---

### The _glReadPixels statement reads a block of pixels from the framebuffer.

#### SYNTAX

`_glReadPixels GLint x , GLint y , GLsizei width , GLsizei height , GLenum format , GLenum type , GLvoid *pixels`
