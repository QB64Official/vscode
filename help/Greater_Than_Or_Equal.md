## Greater Than Or Equal
---
<blockquote>

### The >= condition symbol denotes that a value must be greater than or equal to another value for the condition to be true .

</blockquote>

#### SYNTAX

<blockquote>

`IF x >= 320 THEN PRINT "Right or center of screen"`

</blockquote>
