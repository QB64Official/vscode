## _glGetPixelMapusv
---

### The _glGetPixelMapfv , _glGetPixelMapuiv , and _glGetPixelMapusv statements return the specified pixel map.

#### SYNTAX

`_glGetPixelMapusv GLenum map , GLushort *values`
