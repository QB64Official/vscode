## _glEvalMesh2
---

### The _glEvalMesh2 statement computes a two-dimensional grid of points or lines.

#### SYNTAX

`_glEvalMesh2 GLenum mode , GLint i1 , GLint i2 , GLint j1 , GLint j2`
