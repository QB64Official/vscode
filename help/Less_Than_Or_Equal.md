## Less Than Or Equal
---
<blockquote>

### The <= condition symbol denotes that a value must be less than or equal to another value for the condition to be true .

</blockquote>

#### SYNTAX

<blockquote>

`IF x <= 320 THEN PRINT "Left or center of screen"`

</blockquote>
