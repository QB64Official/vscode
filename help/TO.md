## TO
---

### TO indicates a range of numerical values or an assignment of one value to another.

#### SYNTAX

`DIM array(1 TO 100) FOR i = 1 TO 10 _MAPUNICODE unicode TO asciicode _SETALPHA alpha%, c1& TO c2&`
